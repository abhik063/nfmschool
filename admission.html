Admission Procedure

As on 1.04.2020 the child’s age should be:
1) 3 years for Admission in Nursery
2) 4 years for Admission in Junior K. G.
3) 5 years for Admission in Senior K. G.
4) 6 years for Admission in Grade I

1. The photographs of the child and parent should be affixed at the space provided in the Application Form.
2. Photocopies of all the documents must be attached with the admission form, however original documents will be required for verification at the time of admission.
3. The Application Form submitted at the school reception does not entitle the candidate for admission in school unless confirmed.
4. Incomplete forms are liable to be rejected. Kindly avoid any kind of cutting, fluid markings and over-writing in the form.
5. Admission in Sr. K.G. onwards will be based on the availability of seats & performance in the Entrance Test.
6. Transport facility will be made available to the students, subject to the availability of seats from any particular area. In case, school transport is not available, the parents will have to make their own arrangements. Routes charted out by the school will not be altered.
7. School Hours:
Nur to Sr. Kg: 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 noon (Monday to Saturday)
Classes I – VIII: 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 pm (Monday to Saturday)
